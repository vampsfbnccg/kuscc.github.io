ABOUT

Students' computer Centre(SCC) Lab Breifing

This computer lab is well equiped with internet connected desktop computers for students to carry out educational research while working on their term papers, assignments and projects.

MISSION

The facility was established to provide effective and effecient ICT solutions for academics, research and management operations in Kenyatta University.

SERVICES

Services offered include Students' computer lab accesibility, Smartcard services, Training, Student portal services, Hostel application, Purchasing and Acquisations.

RESOURCES

Accessible resources in the scc computer lab includes internet connected computers, which has One host in every raw while the rest monitors are connected to the N-computing device.

SUPPORT

- Managing ICT helpdesk.
- Administration of University Corporate Email.
- Software upgrade and installation.
- Facilitation of paperless presantation.

LAB RULES

Respect, Responsiblity and Embrace lab rules

- No use of Laptops.
